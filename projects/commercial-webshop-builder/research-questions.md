# Research Questions — First Hypothesis

## Hypothesis

White-label n8n automation templates for AI agencies, n8n consultants, automation freelancers and solopreneurs.

## Core question

Will automation builders pay for practical, reusable workflow templates that help them deliver client projects faster?

## Market questions

1. Are n8n consultants and AI automation agencies already buying templates, starter kits or workflow packs?
2. Which workflow categories are most repeated in client work?
3. What makes a template trustworthy enough to use in client delivery?
4. What documentation must be included?
5. What price points appear in comparable digital products?
6. Which channels contain active buyers rather than only hobbyists?
7. Are there legal or licensing concerns with white-label workflow templates?
8. Which payment and delivery setup is simplest for a Belgium-based seller?
9. Which free or low-cost storefront options are sufficient for a first sale?
10. What are the strongest objections from builders?

## Evidence to collect

- competitor examples;
- pricing examples;
- community discussions;
- buyer objections;
- common workflow requests;
- tool/platform constraints;
- checkout/delivery options;
- signs of paid demand.

## Output requirement

Research must end in a decision:

- build first workflow;
- adjust offer;
- change target audience;
- run another test;
- stop.
