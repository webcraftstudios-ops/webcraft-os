## Status


## Hypothesis

People looking for difficult-to-find classic-car parts may submit a request and may be willing to pay for increased visibility or personal sourcing help.

## Target group

- classic-car owners;
- restorers;
- hobbyists;
- classic-parts suppliers in Belgium and the Netherlands.

## Original offer ideas

- free request during a beta;
- highlighted request at an indicative price;
- personal search help at an indicative price;
- annual specialist or dealer offer.

These were exploratory price ideas. They are not validated prices and do not authorize payment work.

## Original success criteria

- 10 requests;
- 5 suppliers;
- 3 willingness-to-pay signals;
- 1 possible match.

## Original stop criteria

- no requests;
- no suppliers;
- only free interest;
- excessive guarantee or condition risk.

## Result

No completed result or decision was recorded in Spike01. Later phases validated intake mechanics and part of the manual sourcing process, not the original pricing hypotheses.
